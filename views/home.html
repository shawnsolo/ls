Love Through Travel

Having met online, we had to travel to see each other for the first time. Then the second time. By our third encounter, our innate love of all things travel, and the excitement of having a partner to enjoy it with, led us to start meeting in more exotic locations: Oregon, Seattle, Victoria, Cozumel, Chicago, Denver, etc. Almost every meaningful event in the relationship took place in a different city. When deciding on a wedding location, an exciting new destination seemed only natural!

Sharing The Love

Until now, we have only been able to share our travels through computer screens and (overly) verbose stories. For our wedding, this had to change. We wanted to share what brought us love with the ones we love the most; a weekend getaway-vacation to NYC, culminating with our wedding!!! We kept the wedding intimate but are looking forward to seeing friends and family at our open house!
